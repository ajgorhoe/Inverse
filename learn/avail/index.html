INVERSE Availability Information


    A demo version of Inverse is available from time to time on our download page. If the demo version is currently not available, you can contact Inverse staff. You are also kindly invited to post your questions and opinions on our Inverse users board.

The release of a shareware version is planned for  spring 2003, but can be postponed for a later period. Shareware version will have only a part of funcionality of the official version.
