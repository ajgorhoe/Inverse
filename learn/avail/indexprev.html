INVERSE Availability Information


    A demo version of Inverse is now available on our download page. It has full functionality therefore you are kindly invited to test it and post your questions and opinions on our Inverse users board. This demo will expire by the release of the first official version (presumably at the end of the year 2000), but later demo versions will probably be available in periodic intervals.

The release of a shareware version is planned for  spring 2001, but can be postponed for a later period in the case of time problems. Shareware version will have only a part of funcionality of the official version.
