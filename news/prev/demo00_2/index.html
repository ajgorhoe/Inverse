February 26, 2000

INVERSE Demo Release Was Delayed


The release of the Inverse demo was delayed due to technical problems. One reason is that C3M is setting up new home page, but with respect to that a lot of unexpected compatibility problems appeared and  the new home page is not yet operational. Release of the demo is partially bound to the new home page and therefore it can not be released yet. The other problem is that all docuentation could not be prepared on time and we also have problems with setting up a mailing list for the Inverse users.

Presumably the demo version will be available by the end of March. If any additional delay is encountered, there will be a notice in the News section of Inverse home page. Refer also to the demo announcement and availability information.
